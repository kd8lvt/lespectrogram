This app needs microphone access to visualize your voice. Click Start to begin.

What is this?

This is a stupid simple spectrogram utility created by kd8lvt for LesageSinging on YouTube.


How do I use it?

Simply click the "Start" button above, and allow usage of your microphone.


Does my microphone data get sent anywhere?

Only if you supply a valid session passord. If you do, your data will be sent directly to your session host.

Otherwise, all your data stays entirely on your device. Feel free to snoop around the client code using your browser's dev tools :)


What spectrogram library does this use?

The lovely Spectrogram-2v02.js by Dr. A.R.Collins

Feel free to contribute on Github!
